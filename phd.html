CCC PhD programme

CCC will support a developing PhD and early-career researcher programme focused on pathogen genomics, One Health surveillance, source attribution, intervention modelling and vaccine-informed prevention.

Themes

Potential project areas

Pathogen genomics

Population structure, AMR, transmission and reservoir-associated lineages.

Metagenomics

Culture-independent surveillance and genome recovery from complex samples.

Source attribution

Machine learning and probabilistic models for identifying infection sources.

Intervention modelling

Decision-support modelling with local stakeholder knowledge.

Vaccines

Antigen discovery, animal vaccination and upstream prevention strategies.

Implementation

Locally led surveillance, policy translation and sustainable capacity building.
