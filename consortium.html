Consortium

A distributed One Health network of investigators, institutions and implementation partners.

The CCC consortium is a collaborative One Health network spanning clinical, veterinary, environmental, genomic, epidemiological, modelling and public-health expertise.

Public email addresses are not displayed. Personal profile links and photographs will be added only where approved.
