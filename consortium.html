Consortium

A collaborative One Health network across clinical, veterinary, environmental, genomic, epidemiological and public-health expertise.

Consortium membership is currently being verified by site leads and principal investigators.

Confirmed members and participating institutions will be listed here once finalised.

Membership categories

  • Core CCC members: individuals actively involved in CCC design, implementation, analysis, governance, writing or strategic development.
  • Site investigators: country/site partners involved in recruitment, sample collection, laboratory work, surveillance, stakeholder engagement or local implementation.
  • Associated contributors: collaborators contributing methods, analysis, policy, modelling, vaccine expertise or linked projects.
  • Alumni / prior contributors: individuals involved in related GETCampy or precursor work but not currently active in CCC.
Email addresses will not be listed publicly unless explicit permission has been given.
