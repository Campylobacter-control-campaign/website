Workshops and training

CCC will host consortium-wide meetings and training sessions, with a focus on early-career researchers.

Meetings will provide opportunities to share work on enteric disease, pathogen genomics, One Health surveillance, interventions and related topics.

Possible topics

  • enteric disease epidemiology;
  • Campylobacter biology and genomics;
  • One Health surveillance;
  • laboratory methods;
  • source attribution;
  • metagenomics;
  • Bayesian Belief Networks and intervention modelling;
  • vaccine-informed prevention.

Materials

Workshop slides, agendas, practicals and recordings will be listed here where they can be shared publicly.

Some materials may be restricted to consortium members.
